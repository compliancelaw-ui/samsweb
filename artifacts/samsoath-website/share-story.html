Share Your Story

Your story can help someone else feel less alone. No pressure, no judgment.

Why Share Your Story?

💙

Someone Needs to Hear It

Right now, someone is searching for proof they're not alone. Your story could be that proof.

🔓

Healing Through Sharing

Many people find that putting their experience into words helps them process and heal.

🌟

Break the Stigma

Every story shared chips away at the shame and silence that keeps families isolated.

You're in Control

  • ✓ Choose to share anonymously or use your name
  • ✓ Write as much or as little as you want
  • ✓ All stories are reviewed before publishing (within 48 hours)
  • ✓ You can request edits or removal anytime
  • ✓ No story is "too small" or "not enough"

Guidelines for Sharing

✓ Please Do:

  • Be honest about your experience
  • Share what helped or what you wish you'd known
  • Use your own words and voice
  • Mention specific challenges you faced
  • Offer hope if you've found any
  • Be real about the hard parts

✗ Please Don't:

  • Include graphic details of substance use
  • Share others' private information
  • Provide medical or legal advice
  • Use stigmatizing language ("junkie," "addict")
  • Promote specific treatment centers
  • Include triggering content without context

Stigma-Free Language Guide

❌ "Addict" / "Junkie" ✅ "Person with substance use disorder"
❌ "Clean" / "Dirty" ✅ "In recovery" / "Using again"
❌ "Committed suicide" ✅ "Died by suicide"
