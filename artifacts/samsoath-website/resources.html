Harm Reduction Resources

Practical tools to reduce risk and save lives

🩹 Naloxone (Narcan)

Naloxone can reverse opioid overdoses and save lives.

  • Get Naloxone: Available at most pharmacies without prescription in many states
  • Learn how to use it: naloxoneforall.org
  • Free Naloxone: nextdistro.org

🧪 Fentanyl Test Strips

Test drugs for fentanyl to reduce overdose risk.

  • Get test strips: DanceSafe.org
  • How to use: Simple, takes 2 minutes
  • Legal in most states

📞 Never Use Alone Hotline

Call 1-800-484-3731 before using substances alone

  • Volunteer will stay on phone while you use
  • They'll call for help if you become unresponsive
  • Free, anonymous, 24/7
