Meet Frank Sheeder

Frank Sheeder is the founder of Sam's OATH, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization created after losing his son Sam to a tragic accident in September 2025.

When Frank shared Sam's story on LinkedIn, it reached 345,000 people and sparked thousands of responses from families hiding their own struggles with a loved one's substance use or mental health challenges. That post became a movement.

As an attorney and compliance expert, Frank brings a unique perspective on workplace culture, risk management, and the business case for breaking stigma. His talks combine personal story, hard data, and actionable strategies.

Speaking Topics

🏢 What's Hidden Doesn't Heal: Breaking Stigma in the Workplace

Ideal for: Corporate events, HR conferences, leadership teams

Length: 45-60 minutes + Q&A

Key Takeaways:

  • The hidden crisis: 70% of employees are personally affected
  • The business cost of stigma (productivity, turnover, crises)
  • Creating Safe Listener programs
  • Building culture where people don't hide
  • ROI of authenticity and community

💙 Sam's Story: From LinkedIn Post to Movement

Ideal for: Nonprofit events, mental health conferences, community gatherings

Length: 30-45 minutes + Q&A

Key Takeaways:

  • Sam's life and what he taught about community
  • The LinkedIn post that changed everything (345K impressions)
  • Why families hide and the cost of silence
  • How Sam's OATH is changing the conversation
  • What "the opposite of addiction is community" really means

🎓 Substance Use & Mental Health: A Family Perspective

Ideal for: Universities, medical schools, social work programs

Length: 60-90 minutes (can include panel with Annie, Joey, Nancy)

Key Takeaways:

  • What families go through (the lived experience)
  • Why stigma-free language matters
  • Supporting loved ones without enabling
  • Self-care for family members
  • The role of community in recovery

⚖️ Legal & Compliance Perspectives on Workplace Mental Health

Ideal for: Legal conferences, compliance professionals, risk managers

Length: 45-60 minutes + Q&A

Key Takeaways:

  • ADA, FMLA, and mental health accommodations
  • Liability considerations (doing nothing vs. doing something)
  • Safe Listener programs and legal boundaries
  • Documentation and duty of care
  • Proactive support as risk mitigation

🗣️ Panel: The Sheeder Family Perspective

Ideal for: Conferences, community events, fundraisers

Length: 60-75 minutes

Panelists: Frank (father), Annie (sister), Joey (brother), Nancy (stepmother)

Format:

  • Each family member shares their perspective on Sam
  • Q&A with audience
  • Powerful, honest, and deeply moving
  • Shows how the same loss affects each person differently

📊 The Data Behind the Movement

Ideal for: Public health conferences, policy forums, research symposia

Length: 45-60 minutes + Q&A

Key Takeaways:

  • The LinkedIn post analytics (what 345K impressions taught us)
  • Who's taking the OATH (demographics, geography, categories)
  • Stories from thousands of families (themes, patterns)
  • Workplace impact data
  • Scalability and reach potential
