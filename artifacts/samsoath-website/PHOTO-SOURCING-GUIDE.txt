SAM'S OATH - PHOTO SOURCING GUIDE
==================================

You mentioned wanting some stock or AI-generated photos for sections.
Here's where to get high-quality, authentic photos that match the Hopeful Twilight design.

=====================================
STOCK PHOTO SOURCES (FREE & PAID)
=====================================

1. UNSPLASH (FREE)
   https://unsplash.com
   - Completely free, high-quality
   - Search terms to try:
     * "support group" "community" "hands together"
     * "diversity people smiling" "authentic portrait"
     * "mental health" "therapy session" "counseling"
     * "family support" "friends hugging"
     * "hope" "healing" "breakthrough"
   - Hopeful Twilight vibe: Natural light, authentic moments, diverse people

2. PEXELS (FREE)
   https://pexels.com
   - Free, no attribution required
   - Good search terms:
     * "community gathering" "people talking"
     * "workplace support" "office diversity"
     * "recovery" "resilience" "strength"
   
3. PIXELS FREE STOCK (FREE)
   https://www.pixels.com/free-stock-photos
   - Similar to Pexels/Unsplash
   - Filter by "people" and "lifestyle"

4. CANVA (FREE + PRO)
   https://canva.com
   - Free tier has great stock photos
   - Pro tier ($13/month) has even more
   - Can also create graphics, badges, certificates

5. ISTOCK/GETTY (PAID)
   https://istockphoto.com
   - More curated, professional
   - $29+ per image or subscription
   - Better for workplace/professional shots

=====================================
COLOR FILTERS TO MATCH DESIGN
=====================================

If photos don't match Hopeful Twilight colors, you can:

1. Use Canva's Photo Editor:
   - Upload photo
   - Adjust → Color → Tint → Add slight teal/blue overlay
   - Brightness +10 to +20 (to match "hopeful" vibe)

2. Use Photoshop/GIMP:
   - Layer → New Adjustment Layer → Hue/Saturation
   - Shift hues toward blue/teal
   - Increase brightness 10-15%
   - Add slight glow/softness

=====================================
PHOTO STYLE GUIDE
=====================================

DO:
✓ Natural, authentic moments
✓ Diverse people (age, race, gender, background)
✓ Soft, natural lighting
✓ Hopeful expressions (not forced smiles, but genuine)
✓ Cool tones (blues, teals, gentle greens)
✓ Connection shown through body language

DON'T:
✗ Overly staged corporate stock photos
✗ Cheesy "everyone pointing at whiteboard" shots
✗ Isolated individuals looking sad/dramatic
✗ Warm tones (oranges, reds, harsh yellows)
✗ Dark, moody, or overly dramatic lighting
✗ Generic "doctor in white coat" medical imagery

COPYRIGHT & ATTRIBUTION
=====================================

Unsplash/Pexels: No attribution required, but nice to do

ALWAYS:
