What We All Agree On

Though we each knew Sam differently and grieve him in our own ways, we all agree on this:

💙 Sam Was Loved

He knew he was loved, even in his darkest moments. That mattered.

🗣️ Silence Doesn't Help

Hiding our pain doesn't make it go away. It just makes us feel more alone.

🤝 Community Is Everything

Sam taught us that the opposite of addiction isn't sobriety—it's community. Connection heals.

✨ His Legacy Lives On

Through Sam's OATH, his belief in openness and authenticity continues to help families.
