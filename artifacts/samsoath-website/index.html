What is Sam's OATH?

A commitment to break the silence. By taking Sam's OATH, you pledge to:

O

Openness

We speak the truth about our experiences without shame. Hiding only perpetuates stigma.

A

Authenticity

We show up as we are, not who we think we should be. Vulnerability creates real connection.

T

Togetherness

No one heals alone. We support each other through shared experiences and understanding.

H

Healing

It starts when we stop hiding and start connecting. Recovery is possible. Hope is real.

"It's okay not to be okay."

— Sam's reminder to us all

Meet Sam

God was at His creative best when He made Sam. Sam's presence lit up every room. He was handsome, full of adventure and wanderlust, and his brilliance shone in both mind and spirit.

With his authentic and engaging nature, Sam could strike up a conversation with anyone, making them feel seen and heard. Music was a thread that ran through his life. He was fearless and had a need for speed—running, skimming across water, or riding anything with a motor.

Sam faced the hard and often lonely road of substance use disorder with courage and honesty. He confronted his struggles head-on and never shied away from the truth of his journey, even when the world's judgment made it harder to seek help or healing.

"Sam taught us the opposite of addiction is not sobriety; it is community."
