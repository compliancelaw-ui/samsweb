Common Questions

Do I have to use my real name?

No. You can choose to display your full name, first name only, initials, or appear as "Anonymous" on the map.

What if I don't want to give my email?

That's fine. Email is completely optional. You'll still get your certificate and badge on the thank you page regardless.

Can I take the OATH if I'm not directly affected?

Absolutely. The third option—"As a supporter standing with families"—is for allies, friends, and anyone who wants to help end stigma.

Will this go on social media automatically?

No. We give you pre-written posts you can use if you choose, but sharing is completely up to you.

Can I remove my pin later?

Yes. Contact us anytime and we'll remove your pin from the map immediately, no questions asked.

Is this only for substance use disorder?

No. Sam's OATH is for families affected by a loved one's substance use OR mental health challenges. Both are included.
